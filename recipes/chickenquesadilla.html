Chicken Quesadilla

Description

This meal originated from Mexico. This recipe is easy and healthy when you are looking for a quick meal!

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Oil
  • Diced bell pepper
  • Diced onion
  • Garlic
  • Taco seasoning
  • Shredded cheese
  • Flour tortillas

Steps

  1. Chop the chicken into bite sized pieces
  2. Warm the oil over medium-high heat in a pan
  3. Add chicken once oil is hot and add taco seasoning on top
  4. saute the chicken for 5 minutes and then place tortilla in pan
  5. Sprinkle shredded cheese and 1/4 of chicken and veggie mixture
  6. Add more cheese and then fold the tortilla
  7. Cook until each side is crispy and remove from pan
